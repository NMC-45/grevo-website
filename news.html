New Drone Fleet Arrives: AeroCrop Expands to 6 Units

GREVO's AeroCrop programme has doubled its capacity with the arrival of three new multispectral drones, bringing the fleet to six active units. Milestone

FarmPulse IoT Network Crosses 4,200 Acres Milestone

The FarmPulse sensor network — GREVO's backbone IoT infrastructure — has crossed the 4,200-acre coverage milestone, making it Zambia's largest smallholder-focused agricultural IoT deployment. Sensors are now active across all 12 cooperative districts, providing real-time soil data to 1,800+ farmers.

1,800 Farmers Now Using GREVO Mobile App

GREVO's farmer-facing mobile application has reached 1,800 registered users — matching the cooperative's total membership. Programme Update
June 2025

ZATP II Grant Milestone: Phase 2 Complete

GREVO has successfully completed all Phase 2 deliverables under the Zambia Agricultural Transformation Programme (ZATP II), unlocking final grant disbursement and setting the stage for Phase 3 expansion. Phase 2 included the deployment of 320 FarmPulse sensors, training of 800 new members, and establishment of cooperative market linkages in 8 districts.
