Global Goals

UN SDG Alignment

GREVO's work directly advances five United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Here's precisely how our cooperative contributes.

1
No Poverty

Ending Rural Poverty

By increasing average farmer incomes by 87% and distributing ZMW 2.4M+ in cooperative surpluses, GREVO directly lifts member households above subsistence. Our profit-sharing model ensures technology creates shared prosperity rather than concentrating wealth.

2
Zero Hunger

Food Security for All

GREVO's precision agriculture approach has increased maize, soybean, and groundnut yields across 4,200+ acres, directly improving food availability in the Copperbelt. Our crop data informs regional food security planning with the Ministry of Agriculture.

8
Decent Work

Dignified Agricultural Livelihoods

GREVO transforms subsistence farming into dignified, technology-enabled livelihoods. We create direct employment through our drone fleet, IoT installation teams, training programmes, and cooperative administration — with fair wages and skills development.

13
Climate Action

Climate-Smart Agriculture

IoT monitoring enables precise irrigation, reducing water waste by up to 35%. AI recommendations minimise fertiliser overuse and associated greenhouse gas emissions. Drone monitoring detects early crop stress from climate events, helping farmers adapt in real time.

17
Partnerships

Catalysing Collaborative Impact

GREVO exemplifies SDG 17 through active partnerships with timbuktoo, AGRA, Mukuba University, ZATP II, UNDP Zambia, and the Ministry of Agriculture — demonstrating how public-private-cooperative collaboration drives scalable agricultural transformation.
