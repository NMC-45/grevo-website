Why Invest in GREVO?

GREVO represents a rare convergence of proven technology, scalable cooperative infrastructure, and a massive underserved market — Zambia's 1.4 million smallholder farmers.

🌍

Food Security Imperative

Sub-Saharan Africa faces a $20 billion annual food import bill despite having 60% of the world's arable land. GREVO's model directly addresses this opportunity by dramatically increasing smallholder productivity.

📈

Proven, Growing Market

From 48 founding members to 1,800+ in five years — a 37x growth trajectory. Zambia's agricultural sector is projected to grow at 6.2% annually through 2030. GREVO is positioned to capture this growth.

Validated Technology

YieldMind AI achieves 92% forecasting accuracy. FarmPulse covers 4,200 acres. AeroCrop drones survey thousands of acres weekly. The technology stack is proven — now it needs to scale.

Experienced Cooperative Team
