Strategic Partners

Our Partners & Funders

GREVO's success is built on collaborative relationships with leading agricultural, technology, and development organisations across Africa and the world.

timbuktoo

timbuktoo GreenTech Hub

Pan-African accelerator programme under UNDP's timbuktoo initiative, supporting technology-driven climate and agricultural solutions across Africa. GREVO submitted its 2026 application to access mentorship, funding, and scale-up support for our IoT and AI platform.

Accelerator Programme
AGRA

AGRA — Alliance for a Green Revolution in Africa

Continental partnership advancing food security and smallholder farmer prosperity. GREVO aligns with AGRA's mission of sustainable agricultural transformation and has applied for AGRA's Innovation Grant to expand our AI forecasting platform to 10,000 farmers.

Funder & Network
AfDB

African Development Bank — Innovation Fund

The AfDB Agriculture Innovation Fund supports scalable AgriTech solutions across the continent. GREVO is an active applicant for AfDB funding to finance Phase 3 of the FarmPulse network expansion and mobile application 2.0 development.

Funder (Application Active)
MU

Mukuba University

Research partnership with Zambia's Mukuba University School of Agriculture. Joint studies on soil health, climate adaptation, and the effectiveness of precision agriculture interventions — with GREVO farms serving as living research sites.

Research Partner
ZATP II

ZATP II — Zambia Agricultural Transformation Programme

The Zambia Agricultural Transformation Programme (Phase II) has provided critical early-stage grant funding that enabled GREVO's IoT infrastructure, drone fleet acquisition, and YieldMind AI development. Phase 2 milestones were completed in June 2025.

Government Grant Programme
UN FSS

UN Food Systems Summit

GREVO's model of cooperative-led precision agriculture was presented at the UN Food Systems Summit national dialogue in Zambia. This recognition has elevated GREVO's profile with international development partners and potential funders.

Recognition & Network
UNDP

UNDP Zambia

UNDP Zambia's support for digital agriculture and cooperative development aligns directly with GREVO's model. Through the timbuktoo programme and SDG acceleration initiatives, UNDP Zambia has been a key institutional advocate for GREVO's scale-up strategy.

Development Partner
MoA

Ministry of Agriculture — Zambia

GREVO cooperates with the Ministry of Agriculture on farmer registration, crop data reporting, and policy alignment. Our FarmPulse data contributes to the national crop monitoring database, and we participate in the Ministry's extension and farmer support programmes.

Government Partner
