Our Journey

Built in Zambia. Scaling Africa.

Five years of building, proving, and growing our AgriTech platform on the ground.

2020
Cooperative Founded
GREVO registered in Lusaka with 48 founding farmer-members and a vision for technology-driven agriculture.
2021
FarmPulse v1 Launch
First 40 IoT sensors deployed across 3 pilot farms. Yield data collection begins.
2023
Drone Fleet & AI Engine
AeroCrop program launched with 4 drones. YieldMind AI achieves 89% forecast accuracy.
2024
1,800 Farmers Onboarded
Mobile app reaches 1,800 active users. First export shipment with blockchain traceability.
2026
timbuktoo Scale-Up
Pan-African expansion. Target: 10,000 farmers in 5 countries by year-end with timbuktoo GreenTech funding.
