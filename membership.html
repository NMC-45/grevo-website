Member Benefits

Why Join GREVO?

GREVO membership opens the door to Africa's most integrated smallholder farming ecosystem — technology, training, markets, and community in one cooperative.

📡

IoT Sensor Access

Get connected to the FarmPulse network — real-time soil moisture, temperature, and crop health data delivered to your phone. Farm smarter, not harder.

🤖

YieldMind AI Forecasting

Our AI analyses your farm's data and provides personalised planting dates, input recommendations, and yield projections — tailored to your specific land and climate.

🚁

Drone Crop Surveys

AeroCrop drone fleet surveys member farms to detect crop stress, pest pressure, and irrigation needs before problems become visible to the naked eye.

📚

Training in Bemba & English

All GREVO training — from agronomy to financial literacy — is delivered in both English and Bemba, ensuring no language barrier limits your progress.

🌍

Pan-African Market Access

GREVO connects your produce to buyers across Zambia and the wider African market, with blockchain traceability ensuring your goods command premium prices.

💰

Profit Sharing

As a cooperative, GREVO distributes surpluses back to members based on participation and production. Your work builds shared wealth — not someone else's profit.

Who Can Join?

GREVO membership is open to any eligible person committed to transforming agriculture in Zambia. We actively encourage women, youth, and first-time farmers to apply.

  • Zambian citizen (or permanent resident with valid documentation)
  • Smallholder farmer, agribusiness operator, or agricultural professional
  • 18 years of age or older
  • Willing to adopt and engage with modern agricultural technology
  • Committed to the cooperative's values of transparency and shared progress
  • Located in Zambia (priority: Copperbelt, but all provinces welcome)

How to Join

The Membership Process

Simple, transparent, and designed for farmers — not bureaucrats.

1

Complete the Online Application

Fill in the membership form below with your details — name, location, farm size, and crops grown. It takes less than 5 minutes. You can also apply in person at our Luansobe office.

2

Pay the Joining Fee

A once-off registration fee of ZMW 500 is required to process your membership. This covers your GREVO farmer ID card, orientation materials, and initial platform onboarding. Annual membership fees are determined at the AGM.

ZMW 500 — Once-off Registration
3

Attend Farmer Orientation

Join one of our regular orientation sessions at Luansobe or a designated district hub. You'll learn how to use the FarmPulse app, understand your member rights, and meet fellow cooperative members. Conducted in English and Bemba.

4

Receive Your GREVO Farmer ID

Upon completion, you receive your official GREVO Farmer ID — your passport to the full technology ecosystem, market access, drone surveys, and training programmes. Welcome to the cooperative.

Membership Questions

Quick answers for new applicants. See our full FAQ page for more.

Can I join if I don't own a smartphone? +
Yes. While a smartphone enhances your GREVO experience through the mobile app, it is not a requirement for membership. Our field coordinators provide support and data access for members without smartphones, and we run regular in-person training sessions in local community centres and cooperative hubs.
Is membership available to women and youth? +
Absolutely — GREVO actively encourages and prioritises the inclusion of women and young farmers. Over 40% of our members are women. We have specific training tracks and mentorship programmes designed for women and first-time farmers. The cooperative was founded on the principle that every Zambian farmer deserves equal access to technology and markets.
What happens after I submit the form? +
After submitting, our membership coordinator will contact you within 48 hours via WhatsApp or phone call to verify your details, answer any questions, and guide you through payment of the ZMW 500 joining fee. You'll then receive your invitation to the next orientation session and your GREVO Farmer ID upon completion.

Already a Member?

Contact your local district coordinator or reach our central office for any membership-related support, ID replacements, or programme queries.
