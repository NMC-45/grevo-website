Getting to GREVO

Detailed directions from major Zambian cities. We recommend confirming the exact access route via WhatsApp before your visit, as some rural roads may vary by season.

From Mufulira Town Centre

Head north on the Mufulira–Kitwe road, turn left at the Luansobe junction (signposted). Follow the dirt road approximately 3km. GREVO farm entrance is on the right, marked with a green GREVO sign.

~10 km · 15 minutes

From Kitwe City

Take the Kitwe–Mufulira road (T4) heading northeast. Pass Chambishi junction and continue to Mufulira town. Follow the directions from Mufulira Town Centre above.

~45 km · 50 minutes

From Lusaka

Take the Great North Road (Cairo Road) northbound from Lusaka. Continue through Kabwe and Ndola. At Ndola, take the Copperbelt Highway toward Kitwe, then proceed to Mufulira as above.

~600 km · 6–7 hours

From Ndola

Take the Ndola–Mufulira road heading northwest. Follow the Copperbelt town route through Kalulushi. Continue to Mufulira town, then follow Mufulira Town Centre directions above.

~60 km · 1 hour
