How to Order

Simple, fast, and direct — no middlemen, just farm-fresh produce.

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp +260 761 441 893 or email grevomultipurpose@gmail.com. Tell us what product and quantity you need.

2

Confirm Your Order

We confirm availability, agree on quantity, delivery method, and final price. Bulk discounts are available for large orders from NGOs, schools, and restaurants.

3

Pay & Receive

Complete payment via mobile money or agreed method. Collect at our Luansobe farm or arrange local delivery (Mufulira area). Done.

Bulk Orders Welcome

GREVO is proud to supply institutions, processors, and community organisations with large-volume, consistent, quality-checked agricultural produce directly from our cooperative network.

We work with NGOs, government food programmes, schools, supermarkets, restaurants, animal feed mills, and cooking oil producers. Custom packaging and delivery can be arranged for established buyers.
